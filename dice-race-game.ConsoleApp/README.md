## Introduction

A turn-based console racing game where you compete against the CPU across a 30-square board. Roll the dice each turn to advance your piece, trigger special squares, and be the first to reach the finish line.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Turn-based Gameplay:** Player and CPU alternate turns, each rolling a 6-sided dice to advance along the board.

- **Special Squares:** Certain squares trigger events — landing on squares 5, 10, or 15 moves you **3 steps forward**, while landing on squares 7, 13, or 20 sends you **2 steps back**.

- **Extra Turn on Roll of 6:** Rolling a 6 grants an immediate extra turn, keeping the pressure on your opponent.

- **Live Board Display:** The board is printed after each turn, showing both pieces moving along their respective paths in real time.
